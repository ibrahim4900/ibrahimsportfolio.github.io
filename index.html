ABOUT ME

I am a highly experienced professional who excels in executing major email and cross-channel marketing programs from inception to completion. I have a strong background in production, particularly in building and quality assurance (QA) of emails, utilizing platforms like Salesforce Marketing Cloud, EveryAction/NGP VAN, and Luminate. My portfolio includes working with renowned clients such as IBM, Liberty Mutual, UNICEF, Fair Fight, and Habitat for Humanity.

I collaborate closely with a multidisciplinary team, including designers, analysts, and paid media planners, to build and target audiences, conduct A/B testing, and optimize campaigns. My mastery of these platforms allows me to effectively map campaign strategies onto technology, providing valuable guidance on leveraging tools and overcoming technical challenges.

Remaining up-to-date with the latest digital trends is a key part of my skill set. I formulate informed perspectives on how clients can harness these trends to propel their programs forward. Additionally, I take care of email reporting and weekly analytics for clients, ensuring they receive actionable insights from their campaigns.


Beyond my professional expertise, my journey to becoming an email developer has a deeply personal dimension. I battled depression, and my role as an email developer became a lifeline that helped me regain my purpose. I've also embarked on a side project: starting a mental health podcast tailored for men. This venture is a testament to my resilience and commitment to supporting others facing similar challenges.
